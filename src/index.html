Our Story

Our love story began in West Lafayette, Indiana back in 2014. We met through mutual friends during our freshman orientation at Purdue University.

After getting to know each other better throughout the school year, we discovered our mutual love for food and cooking, music, movies, and travel. We went on our first date later that year, and the rest is history!

Five years later and we have many great memories together, including traveling to Italy, graduating Purdue, moving to Seattle, and adopting a feline friend! In June of 2018, Matt popped the question, and Madison said yes! We can’t wait for this exciting new chapter to begin!

Hotel Covington

Like many great things in Kentucky, it began with a horse race. The state’s first modern skyscraper and high-end department store were born from John Coppin’s bet on a longshot—a horse named Knowledge. With big winnings and big dreams, the rest, they say, is history.

Today, Hotel Covington stands as a testament to high stakes, high fashion and high ambition. You can feel it in all 114 guestrooms, unique restaurants, intimate lounge spaces and outdoor courtyards. With vintage touches and modern tailoring, the Hotel Covington sings in style, character and comfort.

We invite you to come, sleep, eat, drink and experience all the local flair and flavor that can only be found here, in one legendary location.
